
安装xadmin:
    pip install https://codeload.github.com/sshwsfc/xadmin/zip/django2

报错：TypeError: render() got an unexpected keyword argument 'renderer'
    解决：修改`xadmin\views\dashboard.py`的`def render(self, name, value, attrs=None)`
        为`def render(self, name, value, attrs=None, renderer=None)`
